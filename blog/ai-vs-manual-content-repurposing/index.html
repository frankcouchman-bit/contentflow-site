4) Quality & Consistency (Guardrails)

  • Voice lock: Provide a short brand voice sample in your prompt to align tone.
  • Structure first: Generate outlines/bullets, then expand. Speeds editing and avoids fluff.
  • Proof pass: Add examples, data, product context, and internal links to related posts.
  • Compliance & review: Keep a quick checklist for claims, trademarks, and CTAs.

6) When Manual Still Wins

  • High-stakes PR or legal reviews.
  • Founder POV essays and sensitive announcements.
  • Heavily technical posts that require original research.

Use AI for derivatives (social/email/FAQs) while you hand-craft the flagship piece.

FAQ

Does AI replace human writers?

No. AI speeds first drafts; humans provide voice, judgment, and proofing.

What about authenticity?

Maintain brand voice by supplying a sample and editing for specifics—examples, data, and product context.

How do I avoid duplicate content?

Adapt per channel, vary intros and headlines, and use canonicals for syndication.
