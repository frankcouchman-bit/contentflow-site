1) What Makes a Great Newsletter?

  • Specific promise: one clear takeaway or story.
  • Skimmable body: short paragraphs, bullets, bold key lines.
  • Single CTA: one action to take.
  • Consistency: familiar format each week.

2) Quickstart: 5 Steps (Featured-Snippet Style)

  1. Pick a source: a blog, podcast, or webinar with clear outcomes.
  2. Paste + generate: drop it into ContentRepurpose.pro for an email draft + subject lines.
  3. Edit for voice: add a story, one tip, and one CTA.
  4. Cross-post: pull 2–3 snippets for LinkedIn/X/IG.
  5. Schedule weekly: keep a simple calendar (strategy guide).

Pro tip: Use the same headline structure across email and social to build recall.

5) FAQ

How can I keep this consistent weekly?

Pick 2–3 formats (e.g., Story+Tip, Playbook, Curated 5). Rotate categories A–D and schedule on the same day each week.

What’s a good length?

120–300 words for quick reads; up to 600–800 for a deep dive. Keep one CTA.

How do I turn a webinar into emails?

Use the outline as a 3–5 email series: recap, 3 takeaways, Q&A, and a case or offer. Add short clips/screenshots.
