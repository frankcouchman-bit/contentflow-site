People also ask

What is content repurposing?

Content repurposing is the process of taking an original piece (a blog, video, or podcast) and transforming it into multiple platform-native formats—e.g., a LinkedIn post, an X thread, an email, and an Instagram caption. Done right, you increase reach and ROI from a single idea while staying consistent across channels.

How do I repurpose content for social media?

Start with your source (blog/notes/URL), extract the core insight, then tailor the voice, length, and structure to each network. Our generator automates this: paste once → get platform-ready drafts for LinkedIn, X, Email, Instagram, and more. See: repurpose a blog post for social and paste once, multi-channel.

Is ContentRepurpose free?

Yes. The Free plan includes 5 generator runs/day and 1 optimizer/day, plus 1 saved preset and 1 Notion/Trello publish per day. Upgrade for batch, scheduler, calendar, and higher caps.

Does it support BYOK (bring your own key)?

Yes—connect your OpenRouter key to control costs and get extra daily headroom per plan. Business plans increase BYOK caps the most for teams.

What’s the best content repurposing strategy?

Start with pillar content, then repurpose into 5–7 formats that fit audience intent. Use consistent hooks, CTAs, and internal links back to the pillar. Helpful reads: strategy guide, repurposing for SEO, workflows.
