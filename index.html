FAQs: Content Repurposing & SEO

How does AI content repurposing improve SEO?

Repurpose content into multiple formats (LinkedIn, X, Email, Instagram) to capture broader search intent and earn links. Our optimizer adds headings, meta, and FAQs with JSON-LD to help pages rank for content repurposing and repurpose content keywords.

Can I publish directly to Notion or Trello?

Yes. Connect once under Integrations, then export drafts with one click. Free gets 1/day, Starter 5/day, Creator & Business unlimited.

What’s the difference between Generator and Article Optimizer?

Generator turns one idea into channel-native drafts. Article Optimizer upgrades existing articles for CTR and SEO (headings, A/B titles, meta, Reddit TL;DR, optional LinkedIn HTML).

Does it support BYOK?

Yes. Bring your own API keys (OpenRouter compatible) for cost control. Free plan: 5/day. Paid: higher daily allowances and caps.
