Content Repurposing FAQs

Answers for people searching for “content repurpose”, “repurposing content”, “social media generator”, and “AI content creation tool”.

What is content repurposing?

It’s turning one asset (blog post, notes, podcast) into multiple platform-native pieces: LinkedIn, X, Email, Instagram, Reddit, Facebook, Quora, and TikTok. Our generator preserves voice and intent while optimizing length, hooks, hashtags, and CTAs.

How is this different from generic AI?

We’re a focused content repurpose tool—not a chat box. Paste once, choose channels, and get platform-ready drafts + optional LinkedIn Article. Presets, batch, scheduler/calendar, Notion/Trello export, and BYOK make it production-ready.

Will this help SEO?

Yes. The Article Optimizer adds headings, titles/meta, FAQs (JSON-LD), alt-text hints, and A/B titles to improve click-through and on-page structure for your target keywords.

Do I need to sign in?

Guest preview is 5 runs/month (IP-based). Sign in for 5/day, access the Article Optimizer, saved presets, and higher caps on paid plans.

What’s BYOK?

Bring Your Own OpenRouter key to run with your preferred model/provider privately and cheaply. Free plan includes 5 BYOK runs/day; higher plans raise BYOK caps.

Can I export to my stack?

Yes—one-click Notion/Trello. “Post now” opens X/Reddit/LinkedIn/email draft windows with your latest content.
