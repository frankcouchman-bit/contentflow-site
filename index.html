What is AI content repurposing?

AI content repurposing turns one source—like a blog, email, or post—into platform-ready drafts for LinkedIn, X, Instagram, and Email. ContentRepurpose works like a focused content repurposing tool: paste once and get channel-specific copy, then export to Notion and Trello for review. It’s a fast way to test content repurposing ideas, keep tone consistent with presets, and build a repeatable workflow for your team.

From email to Trello or Notion (in one click)

Turn email or blog drafts into a “repurpose project” you can manage. Send outputs to Trello as cards or export to Notion pages for approvals. Organize status, assignees, and deadlines while your writers polish headlines and CTAs. It keeps ideation, editing, and scheduling in one flow—so you can repurpose content quickly and keep your calendar full.

          Content Repurposing Tool — FAQs
          

    
    
          
      
          
        
          What is AI content repurposing?
          
        
          
          AI content repurposing turns one source—like a blog, email, or social post—into
          ready-to-edit drafts for LinkedIn, X, Instagram, and
          Email. ContentRepurpose is a focused content repurposing tool that keeps tone on-brand,
          suggests hooks, and structures posts for scannability. When you’re done, export to
          Notion or Trello with one click. Looking for content repurposing ideas?
          Start from any paragraph—the generator will create channels, captions, and email copy.
        
          
      
          
      
          
        
          From email to Trello or Notion (in one click)
          
        
          
          Turn a rough email into a cross-channel draft, then file it to a board or doc instantly.
          With Trello export, drafts slot into your editorial lists; with Notion export, your team
          can comment and approve. It’s a simple workflow for any repurpose project—capture an idea,
          generate variants, and track publishing. When you need scale, upgrade plans unlock batch inputs.
        
          
      
          
    
          

    
    
          
      
          
        How do I repurpose a blog post into LinkedIn, X, Instagram and Email?
        
          Paste the blog excerpt, click Generate, and you’ll get a LinkedIn post, two short X variants, an email subject/body, and an Instagram caption + hashtags.
          
      
          
      
          
        Can I export content to Notion or Trello?
        
          Yes. Creator and Business plans export to Notion and send cards to Trello boards.
          
      
          
      
          
        Does ContentRepurpose support BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)?
        
          Yes. Free supports 5/day on your BYOK; paid plans increase monthly BYOK allowances.
          
      
          
      
          
        What are effective content repurposing ideas for SEO?
        
          Turn long-form into social snippets, email intros, and carousel hooks; include keywords naturally to improve CTR and win long-tails.
          
      
          
      
          
        Can I turn one idea into a repurpose project pack?
        
          Yes. Creator+ can export a project pack and organize drafts into Notion/Trello for multi-channel publishing.
          
      
          
      
          
        How many AI content repurposing runs can I do on the free plan?
        
          Demo: 5/month (no login). Signed-in free: 5/day. BYOK: 5/day.
          
      
          
      
          
        What’s the difference between Solo Creator, Creator, and Business?
        
          Solo Creator adds batch inputs and 10 presets. Creator unlocks integrations and unlimited presets. Business adds seats and white-label export.
          
      
          
      
          
        Does it support Buffer (queueing) and a shared content calendar?
        
          Buffer queueing is coming soon. Shared calendar appears on Business as we roll out.
