          Content Repurposing Tool — FAQs
          
    
          
      
          
        Is it free?
        
          Yes. Demo is 5/month by IP. Signed-in free is 5/day. BYOK is supported.
          
      
          
      
          
        What channels are supported?
        
          LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Email and Instagram—plus Notion/Trello export and Buffer (Creator/Business).
          
      
          
      
          
        How does ContentRepurpose.pro help SEO?
        
          Our content repurposing AI produces keyword-aware snippets that improve click-through from social/email and reinforce topical relevance for your site.
          
      
          
      
          
        Do you support BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)?
        
          Yes. Limits: Free 5/day • Solo Creator 50/month (+5 daily) • Creator 150/month (+10 daily) • Business 300/month (+30 daily).
          
      
          
      
          
        Can I publish instantly?
        
          Open X composer, copy to LinkedIn, preview/send email, or jump to Meta Creator Studio for IG.
          
      
          
      
          
        What’s included in paid plans?
        
          Higher monthly generations, saved presets, integrations, and faster lanes. See pricing for details.
          
      
          

      
      
          
        What is a content repurposing tool?
        
          It lets you repurpose content from one source into platform-ready drafts. Our tool automates LinkedIn, X, Email & IG with consistent tone and keywords.
          
      
          
      
          
        How does content repurposing AI work?
        
          You paste text or a link. The AI content repurposing engine extracts key points, applies channel rules, and returns drafts you can edit or export.
          
      
          
      
          
        Do you provide content repurposing ideas?
        
          Yes—templates and prompts inside the app generate fresh content repurposing ideas for posts, threads, emails, and captions.
          
      
          
      
          
        Can I run a repurpose project in batches?
        
          Yes. Starter/Creator/Business plans include batch inputs to run a whole repurpose project from a list of ideas or URLs.
          
      
          
      
          
        Is this the right tool to repurpose content AI-style?
        
          If you need speed and consistency, our repurpose content AI workflow is built to keep your brand voice while saving hours each week.
          
      
          
      
          
        Do you support teams and agencies?
        
          Yes—Business includes seats, white-label export, and shared presets. Great for agencies coordinating multi-channel campaigns.
